RESIW

We are RESIW, an up-and-coming graduate employer in the professional services industry with over 2000 employees worldwide. Our HQ is located in London, whilst also having offices in other major cities across North America, Europe, and Asia. Our mission is to empower both individuals and businesses to achieve financial success through personalised, accessible professional services.

Our services include:
  • audit and assurance;
  • tax planning and compliance;
  • risk and wealth management;
  • corporate finance;
  • compliance and financial technology solutions.

The Audit Graduate Role

For this role, you will:
  • be based in Broadgate, London (with limited prospects of relocation to other offices for the duration of the graduate scheme);
  • join a cohort of 50 audit graduates and go straight into working with the Audit team;
  • be given a senior mentor to support you pastorally and professionally;
  • get stuck into projects from the get go, working on client site across a range of industries;
  • get exposure to different areas of audit through cycles of experience looking at both financial and non financial information, to give you a greater variety of experience and depth of understanding of the clients;
  • have the opportunity to work towards a Chartered Accountant (ACA or CA) qualification;
  • receive monthly learning and development sessions.
We are looking for:
  • Students that would not typically consider an career in audit, such as those from a Humanities or STEM background;
  • Analytical thinkers and problem solvers;
  • People with financial curiosity and an eagerness to learn;
  • Those with an ability to digest complex information and present it back in a clear and concise manner.
